FRUITS AND VEGETABLES

Fruits

Fruits are the sweet and fleshy product of a tree or
other plant that contains seed/seeds that can be eaten as food

Vegetables

There are plants or part of a plant that can be used as food.
Parts of a plant that can be used as food include;
Leaves, Stems, Roots, Tubers, Bulbs, Flowers.



Below are examples of vegetables;

  • Lettuce
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Potato

Below are examples of fruits;

  1. Oranges
  2. Tangerine
  3. Lemon
  4. Lime
